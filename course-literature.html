About This Course

This course introduces students to Arabic literature from classical poetry to modern writings, building deep reading and analytical skills.

Course Modules

01

Classical Poetry

Study pre-Islamic and classical Arabic poetry, its rhythm, themes, and literary beauty.

02

Classical Prose

Explore speeches, writings, and texts from early Arabic scholars and historians.

03

Modern Literature

Study modern Arabic novels, short stories, and contemporary literary movements.

04

Literary Analysis

Learn to analyze poetry and prose using themes, style, and rhetorical techniques.

05

Creative Writing

Practice writing expressive Arabic texts inspired by classical and modern styles.
