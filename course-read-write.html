About This Course

This course is designed for absolute beginners who want to learn Arabic reading and writing step by step with clarity and ease.

Course Modules

01

Module 1: Arabic Alphabet

Introduction to Arabic letters, pronunciation, and writing foundations.

02

Module 2: Letter Forms

Learn how Arabic letters change shape depending on their position in a word.

03

Module 3: Vowels & Harakāt

Learn short vowels, sukoon, tanween, and long vowel sounds.

04

Module 4: Reading Practice

Practice reading simple words and short sentences with guidance.

05

Module 5: Writing Practice

Develop handwriting skills through structured writing exercises.
